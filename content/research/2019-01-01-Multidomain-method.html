High-throughput sequencing and single-cell technologies have revolutionized the quantification of molecular biomarkers at the genome-level. The data produced by these techniques are complex in nature because of high-dimensionality, heterogeneity, multi-domain, and sparsity.

Data structure for multi-domain genome-level data

First, I am working on establishing a data structure for multi-domain genome-level data in R/Bioconductor.

Statistical method for multi-domain genome-level data

Second, I focus on developing probabilistic modeling for multi-domain genome-level data in R/Bioconductor/Stan.
